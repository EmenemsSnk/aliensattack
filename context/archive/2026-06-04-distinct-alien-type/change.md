## Notes

- Planning decisions: one special alien appears every 2 waves from wave 2 onward; it has 2 HP, 1.2x wave-relative vertical speed, horizontal movement with a 2% per-tick random direction change, about 2x normal firing frequency, and a distinct sprite.
- The global alien-missile cap increases from 2 to 5. Special aliens retain standard kill score, rapid-fire drop chance, collision damage, invasion behavior, and wave-completion semantics.
